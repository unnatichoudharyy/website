Redesigned Album Coverof AC/DC


Project Brief:

AC/DC is a legendary rock band known for their electrifying music and captivating performances. Their album art is equally iconic, representing the raw energy and rebellious spirit of their music. The cover of "Back in Black" features a bold yellow logo against a black background, while "Highway to Hell" showcases a devilish figure engulfed in flames. These album covers, along with their recognizable lightning bolt logo, perfectly capture the essence of AC/DC's rock 'n' roll legacy. This vinyl cover designed is an attempt to capture the essence of their music, which often explored Rock and Punk themes.

My Redesign

AAC/DC's "Highway to Hell" album cover redesign exudes a rebellious spirit and electrifying energy that perfectly matches the band's music. The design features a vibrant and eye-catching colour palette, as well as a meticulously detailed illustration of the road that leads straight into the depths of hell. The album cover's use of bold and bright colours immediately catches the eye and conveys a sense of intensity and audacity. These vibrant colours evoke a sense of excitement and rebellion, reflecting the band's electrifying sound and proclivity for pushing boundaries. The intricate depiction of the road to hell takes centre stage, commanding attention with its intricate depiction.Every detail of the road is meticulously depicted, capturing the essence of a perilous journey. The imagery represents the band's exploration of darker themes and the consequences of living life on the edge
